Best Hostels In Nairobi

>

Nairobi is the capital and largest city of Kenya, as well as a major hub for education, business, culture, and tourism in East Africa. Nairobi hosts many colleges and universities that attract people from all over the country and the region. However, finding a suitable and affordable accommodation in Nairobi   can be a challenge for many individuals, especially those who are new to the city. Thatâ€™s why many students opt for hostels, which are shared living spaces that offer basic facilities and services at a lower cost than other types of accommodation.

But,how do you choose the best hostel. Here are some tips to help you find the ideal hostel for your needs and preference:

    Location

    The location of your hostel will affect your convenience, comfort, and safety. You should choose a hostel that is close to your campus or public transport, as well as other facilities such as shops, markets, restaurants, banks, etc. You should also consider the environment and neighborhood of your hostel, and avoid areas that are noisy, polluted, crowded, or prone to crime. You can use online platforms such as Google Maps or Dorms.com to search for hstels in diferernt locations in Nairobi.

    Price

    The price of your hostel will depend on the size, type, and condition of the room, as well as the facilities and services offered by the hostel. You should compare different options and find the one that fits your budget and expectations. You should also check for any hidden fees or charges that may apply, such as deposits, utilities, internet, laundry, etc. You can use online platforms such as #Jiji , #Property24, or #BuyRentKenya to search for hostels with different prices in Nairobi.

    Quality

    The quality of your hostel will affect your comfort, health, and satisfaction. You should choose a hostel that is clean, well-maintained, and spacious. You should also check the condition and functionality of the furniture, appliances, fixtures, and fittings in your room and in the common areas. You should also look for reviews and ratings from previous or current guests to get an idea of the quality of the hostel. You can use online platforms such as #Dorms.com or #Qejani.co.ke to search for hostels with different quality ratings in Nairobi.

    Security

    The security of your hostel will affect your safety and peace of mind. You should choose a hostel that has adequate security measures such as locks, alarms, guards, cameras, etc. You should also check the reputation and record of the hostel and its management regarding security issues. You should also follow some basic safety precautions such as keeping your valuables safe, locking your door, avoiding strangers, etc. You can use online platforms such as Dorms.com or Qejani.co.ke to search for hostels with different security features in Nairobi.

    Amenities

    The amenities of your hostel will affect your convenience and enjoyment. You should choose a hostel that has essential amenities such as water, electricity, internet, parking, etc., as well as additional amenities such as cafeteria, study area, social space, laundry service, etc. You should also check the availability and quality of these amenities and whether they are included in the price or not. You can use online platforms such as Dorms.com2 or Qejani.co.ke to search for hostels with different amenities in Nairobi.

    Community

    The community of your hostel will affect your social life and learning experience.You should choose a hostel that has a diverse and friendly community of individuals from different backgrounds and interests. You should also look for opportunities to interact and network with other students through events, activities, clubs, etc. You should also respect the rules and norms of the hostel and its community and avoid any conflicts or misunderstandings. You can use online platforms such as Dorms.com or Qejani.co.ke to search for hostels with different community profiles in Nairobi.

    These are some of the tips to help you choose the best hostel in Nairobi. However, you should also consider your personal preference and intuition when making a decision. You should always do your research and visit the hostels in person before booking or moving in. You should also communicate with the hostel management and staff to clarify any doubts or issues that may arise.
